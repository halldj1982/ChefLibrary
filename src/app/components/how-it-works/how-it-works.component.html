Detailed Process

  1. Upload Your Recipe

    Take a photo of any recipe - from cookbooks, recipe cards, or even handwritten notes. Upload it to our platform with a simple click.

  2. AI Processing

    Our advanced AI analyzes the image, recognizes text, and intelligently structures it into ingredients, instructions, and other recipe components.

  3. Review and Edit

    Review the extracted recipe, make any necessary edits, and add additional details like cooking time, servings, or tags.

  4. Save to Your Collection

    Once you're satisfied, save the recipe to your personal collection where it becomes searchable and ready to use.

Features

  • Smart Search

    Find recipes by ingredients, cooking methods, dietary restrictions, or even vague descriptions like "quick dinner ideas".

  • Meal Planning

    Create weekly meal plans by dragging and dropping recipes into a calendar view. Generate shopping lists automatically.

  • Recipe Scaling

    Automatically adjust ingredient quantities based on your desired number of servings.

  • Cooking Mode

    A distraction-free view of your recipe while cooking, with step-by-step instructions and timers.
